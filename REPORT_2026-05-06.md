### 4.1 Near-term (T+0 to 2026-06-30, 55 days)

1. US sends supplementary evidence to SRE (P ≈ 60% within window)
2. SRE forwards to FGR; FGR finds urgency (P ≈ 25% conditional)
3. FGR petitions juez de control; warrant issued (P ≈ 50% conditional)
4. Rocha detained physically (P ≈ 40% conditional — he has GN protection, may surrender voluntarily or contest)

## 6. EVIDENCE QUALITY ASSESSMENT (US case)

**Strengths (US case):**
- Three high-value cooperators with signed plea deals: Iván Guzmán López, Ovidio Guzmán López (both Chapitos), and Ismael "El Mayo" Zambada.
- Specific factual allegations (pre-2021 election meeting with armed sicarios, drugs+weapons charges, "millions of dollars" in bribes).
- DOJ unsealed = political commitment; case is hard to walk back.

**Weaknesses (US case):**
- Cooperator testimony alone is rarely sufficient for MX extradition under Mexican-Constitutional standard ("pruebas contundentes").
- No documentary corroboration disclosed publicly (financial records, intercepts, photos).
- García Harfuch (MX SSP) publicly stated GoMX had "no prior suspicion" — defensive posture from federal exec.
- Politicized timing (2-yr-old Zambada cooperation surfacing now) gives MX cover to demand more.

**Tipping-point catalyst:** declassification of cooperator affidavits or release of intercepts. Until then, the case is procedurally stuck.

## 7. POLITICAL ECONOMY

**Sheinbaum's incentive structure:**
- Cost of extraditing Rocha: signal of US judicial supremacy over MX sovereignty; alienates Morena base; precedent for additional Morena extraditions (Marina del Pilar, Durazo, Villarreal rumors).
- Cost of NOT extraditing: friction with Trump admin; potential trade/tariff/security retaliation.
- Optimal play: drag process, demand evidence, prosecute domestically (or appear to). High-confidence scenario.

**Trump admin incentive:**
- Cost of pressing hard now: blow up USMCA review, midterm-cycle distraction.
- Cost of dropping: looks soft on cartels (designated FTOs).
- Optimal play: maintain indictment as leverage; don't insist on physical transfer in Q2-Q3.

**Equilibrium:** *cold case status* through end-2026. Both sides win optically without action.
