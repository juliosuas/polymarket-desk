# Code Of Conduct

Polymarket Desk should be a useful, direct, and welcoming place to build prediction-market tooling.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific in issues, reviews, and discussions.
- Critique code, product decisions, and market assumptions without personal attacks.
- Make space for newcomers and explain context when it helps.
- Keep financial, political, and market discussions grounded in product behavior and data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, slurs, or targeted personal attacks.
- Sharing private credentials, doxxing, or sensitive personal information.
- Spam, low-effort promotion, or misleading claims about trading outcomes.
- Attempts to use the project to evade platform rules, laws, or account restrictions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, and pull requests that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocking from the project.
